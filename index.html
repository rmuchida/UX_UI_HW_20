Goldie and Papi's Animal Rescue

Non-Profit Website Redesign

Why Goldie and Papi's Animal Rescue?

Goldie and Papi's Animal Rescue is a non-profit dedicated to rescuing and providing loving forever and foster homes for loyal animals. Not only do all animals deserve the feeling of quality love but many people also want to enjoy the presence of a loyal companion. Our re-design of this website will help provide a supportive and efficient experience for visitors thinking about adopting or fostering animals.

Project Goal

Redesign Goldie and Papi's Animal Rescue site to help visitors have a more user friendly experience while navigating the complexities that come with adopting or fostering an animal.

Problem Statement

People who seek a loyal companion would like an efficient method of finding information on animals that are available for adoption/foster.

Solution

Completely Redesign the current website with an entirely new navigation system as well as an added feature of a Pet Match Quiz to give visitors an opportunity to find their perfect loyal companion.
